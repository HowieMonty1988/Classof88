Class of 88 Story Recorder

This page is for collecting real stories and memories from our high school years. There is no writing required. Just press record and tell your story in your own words.

Your story can be funny, meaningful, serious, or simple. One memory or several moments. There is no time limit.

How this works

  1. Fill in your name and optional contact information.
  2. Press Start Recording.
  3. Tell your story at your own pace.
  4. Press Stop when finished.
  5. Download your recording or upload it when prompted.

If you want your story kept private and not used in a book, say that clearly at the start of your recording.

Contact information is only used if clarification is needed.
